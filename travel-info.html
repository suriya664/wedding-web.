Travel Information

Everything you need to know about getting here

By Air

The nearest airport is City International Airport, located 20 miles from the venue. Taxi and rental car services are available at the airport.

Airport Code: CTY

By Train

The city train station is 5 miles from the venue. Regular train services connect to major cities.

Station: City Central Station

By Car

The venue is easily accessible by car. Ample parking is available on-site. Use GPS coordinates or follow the directions provided.

Address: 123 Wedding Street, City, Country

Public Transport

Bus routes 101 and 205 stop near the venue. Check local transit schedules for the most up-to-date information.

Nearest Stop: Wedding Hall Stop
